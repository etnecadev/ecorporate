AMEC TB560

The TB-560 portable AIS Class B Tracking Beacon is a simplified “AIS Class B” device that offers efficient and cost effective vessel tracking solution. With this device installed onboard, the port authorities and or vessel owners can locate, identify, and/or monitor vessels within the AIS VHF transmission range.

The TB-560 is activated when it´s mounted into a dedicated bracket. The unit’s temper proof feature intelligently prevents unauthorized transferring between vessels. TB560 uses rechargeable battery and can operate up to five (5) working days. The device has built-in VHF and GPS antennas and can transmit SRM in case of emergency. It is compact, lightweight, robust and easy to install even for smaller vessels without onboard power supply.

Em-trak I100

The Identifier I100-X is a ruggedized, fully self-contained AIS Class B vessel tracking transponder, ideal for small vessel tracking and monitoring. I100-X can power itself using its own internal rapid recharge battery (+120hrs continuous operation), or be connected directly to another onboard power source such as engine, battery or solar with the battery then acting as a backup. I100-X is fully water and weather proof (IPx8 certified) and ruggedized to operate reliably without maintenance in even the harshest of environments - extreme heat/cold, humidity, shock and vibration.

  • Certified AIS Class B Transponder
  • 5 day continuous use from its internal battery or connect directly to another onboard power source such as engine, battery or solar for a permanent installation
  • Takes only 5 hours to completely recharge
  • Rugged IPx8 design ensures operational performance
